Our Story

A Taste of Tradition,
Right Here in Kulti

At The London Bakery, we believe great food should feel like home — even if that home is thousands of miles away. Nestled at College More on Grand Trunk Road, we bring the soul of London's vibrant food culture to the heart of Kulti.

Every item on our menu is crafted with hand-picked ingredients and time-honoured recipes refined to suit your city's palate. From our signature chaaps to stone-baked pizzas, every bite tells a story worth savouring.

Whether you're dropping in for a quick bite or staying for a leisurely evening, our team is here to make every visit unforgettable. Kulti's favourite — and proudly yours.
